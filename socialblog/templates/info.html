About Our Social Blog

SocialBlog is a friendly place where people can share their thoughts, ideas, stories, and experiences with others.

Our goal is to create a simple and welcoming online space where users can write blog posts, read content from other people, and connect through meaningful conversations. Whether you want to share personal stories, learning experiences, opinions, or helpful tips, this blog gives you a space to express yourself.

This project is also built to practice web development using Flask, templates, routes, user authentication, and database management.
